Types of Exams

Abdominal Ultrasound

Comprehensive imaging of abdominal organs including liver, kidney, pancreas, and spleen.

Obstetric Ultrasound

Specialized ultrasound for pregnancy monitoring and fetal development assessment.

Thyroid Ultrasound

Detailed imaging of the thyroid gland to detect nodules and evaluate thyroid function.

Vascular Ultrasound

Doppler ultrasound to assess blood flow and detect vascular abnormalities.

Musculoskeletal Ultrasound

Imaging of joints, tendons, and muscles to diagnose injuries and inflammation.

Cardiac Ultrasound

Echocardiography to evaluate heart structure and function.
