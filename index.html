About LOGUS

LOGUS is a clinic in Ribeirão Preto specializing in general ultrasound that combines medical expertise, cutting-edge technology, and personalized care. Our mission is to offer accurate, safe exams conducted by professionals certified by the Brazilian College of Radiology.

With a focus on high diagnostic sensitivity, the clinic seeks to provide a welcoming and informative experience, where each patient is treated with respect, attentiveness, and transparency.

LOGUS offers all types of ultrasound exams, with strict quality protocols and a comfortable environment, designed to provide safety from the first contact.

Our Values

  • Technical excellence: scientific rigor, diagnostic depth, and attention to detail.
  • Humanization: each exam is a relationship of care, listening, and respect.
  • Transparency: total clarity for doctors and patients.
  • Professionalism: ethics, commitment, and responsibility in diagnosis.
  • Accuracy: quality over volume; adequate time, attentive gaze.

Types of Exams

Women's Health Ultrasound

Comprehensive imaging for women's health including transvaginal, breast and axillary ultrasound.

Obstetric Ultrasound

Specialized ultrasound including 4D ultrasound, simple exams, morphological assessments and obstetric Doppler.

Men's Health Ultrasound

Detailed imaging for men's health including prostate, penile ultrasound, penile Doppler, and pharmacologically-induced erection test.

oncology

Internal Medicine Ultrasound

Exams related to liver, gallbladder, pancreas, spleen, and urinary system pathologies.

Vascular Ultrasound

Doppler ultrasound to assess venous and arterial blood flow, including carotid imaging.

Musculoskeletal Ultrasound

Imaging of joints, muscles, and tendons to diagnose injuries and inflammation.

Dermatological Ultrasound

Specialized ultrasound imaging for skin and dermatological conditions.

Elastography

Advanced ultrasound technique to assess tissue elasticity and rigidity.
